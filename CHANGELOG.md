## 2026-07-21

### Camera Scanner Reliability Fix

### Files Modified

- `frontend/src/components/AssetQrScanner.tsx`
- `frontend/src/components/QrCode.tsx`
- `AI_CHANGELOG.md`
- `CHANGELOG.md`

### Reason

The QR camera flow depended on native `BarcodeDetector`, which is not consistently available across browsers/devices, and the PSA QR label used a custom local renderer that could be harder for real scanners to decode.

### Summary

- Replaced native `BarcodeDetector` scanning with the installed `@zxing/browser` `BrowserQRCodeReader`.
- Let ZXing manage camera stream startup, preview, scanning, and QR decode callbacks.
- Added a clear insecure-LAN warning when phone browsers block camera access over non-HTTPS network URLs.
- Replaced the custom QR matrix renderer with ZXing `BrowserQRCodeSvgWriter`.
- Kept backend lookup unchanged through the existing `/api/v1/assets/scan` flow.

### Impact

- Camera scanning should work on more devices/browsers.
- Printed/displayed PSA QR labels are now generated by the same QR library family used by the scanner.
- Manual fallback remains available if a browser blocks camera permission or LAN HTTP camera access.
- Frontend production build passes after the change.
